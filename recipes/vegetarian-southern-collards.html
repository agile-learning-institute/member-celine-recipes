Vegetarian Southern Collards

Home

Description

This recipe is a vegetarian take on the classic southern meat-seasoned dish. You won't find yourself missing the meat flavor as it is seasoned in a way that adds so much more depth and flavor. The collards will melt in your mouth and make you wish you could live on vinegear and greens.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 diced white onion
  • 1 quart of veggie broth
  • 32 oz of chopped collards
  • 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar
  • Spices: Salt, pepper, red pepper flakes, cajun seasoning, onion powder and smoked garlic powder
  • 2 gloves of diced garlic

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil on medium heat in a large stock pot, then add the diced onion. Cook until slightly charred, then add the diced garlic.
  2. Add the veggie broth and spices then bring to a boil.
  3. Add the collards, cover, then turn down the heat and let simmer for 2 hours. 45 minutes is the minimum, but the longer, the more flavor will be unleashed.
  4. Add the apple cider vingear and adjust the spices as necessary.
